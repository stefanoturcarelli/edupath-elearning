About us

Meet the Team Behind EduPath

We are Adeniyi, Marcelo, and Stefano, three dedicated software development students who first crossed paths in college. Our shared vision and passion for online learning inspired us to create EduPath.

During our early days, we navigated the challenges of coursework while simultaneously developing EduPath. Each of us brought a unique set of skills to the table: Adeniyi's visionary ideas, Marcelo's technical expertise, and Stefano's business acumen. Despite the obstacles we faced, our unwavering belief in the transformative power of education kept us moving forward.

Our journey began with the launch of our first course, an introduction to coding, which received an overwhelming response from eager learners. This initial success fueled our determination to expand and offer a diverse range of courses across various domains.

Our Commitment

At EduPath, we are committed to providing the best learning experience possible. We understand that education is a lifelong journey, and we're here to guide and support you every step of the way.

Why Choose EduPath?

Our team combines Adeniyi's visionary ideas, Marcelo's technical prowess, and Stefano's business acumen to deliver high-quality educational content that meets industry standards. We believe in continuous learning and improvement. We are constantly evolving our courses to incorporate the latest industry trends and best practices.
