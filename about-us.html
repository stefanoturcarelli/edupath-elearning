About us

We’re Adeniyi, Marcelo, and Stefano, three software development students who met in college. We saw the potential of online learning and created EduPath. In the early days, we juggled coursework while developing EduPath. Each of us brought unique skills - Alex’s visionary ideas, Bailey’s technical expertise, and Charlie’s business acumen. Despite challenges, our belief in the transformative power of education kept us going. The launch of our first course, an introduction to coding, received an overwhelming response. Today, EduPath offers a wide array of courses across various domains. We’re continuously evolving to provide the best learning experience. Join us on this exciting journey.
