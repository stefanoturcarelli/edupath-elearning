About EduPath

Welcome to our online school, a place where knowledge knows no boundaries and curiosity is the compass guiding our educational journey. Nestled in the virtual realm, our institution stands as a beacon of learning, harnessing the power of technology to bring the world's wealth of knowledge to your fingertips. We invite you to embark on a transformative educational odyssey, where innovation and dedication converge to shape a brighter future.

Through our virtual classrooms, you'll traverse a landscape of diverse subjects, from mathematics to literature, science to the arts, where interactive lessons, engaging discussions, and real-world applications await. Join us on this educational adventure, and let's pave the way to a brighter tomorrow, one lesson at a time.

About Team

We’re Shella, Marcelo, and Stefano, three software development students who met in college. We saw the potential of online learning and created EduPath. In the early days, we juggled coursework while developing EduPath. Each of us brought unique skills - Alex’s visionary ideas, Bailey’s technical expertise, and Charlie’s business acumen. Despite challenges, our belief in the transformative power of education kept us going. The launch of our first course, an introduction to coding, received an overwhelming response. Today, EduPath offers a wide array of courses across various domains. We’re continuously evolving to provide the best learning experience. Join us on this exciting journey.
