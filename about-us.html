About EduPath

Meet the minds behind EduPath: Richard, Marcelo, and Stefano, software development students who came together during their college years. United by a shared vision for the potential of online learning, we embarked on the journey of creating EduPath. During those early days, we deftly balanced our coursework while nurturing EduPath's growth, with each of us contributing a unique set of skills: Richard's visionary ideas, Marcelo's technical expertise, and Stefano's business acumen.

Despite the inevitable challenges, our unwavering belief in the transformative power of education propelled us forward. The debut of our inaugural course, an introduction to coding, garnered an overwhelmingly positive response.
